What is Couples Connect?

There’s a buzz around Central’s Charlotte campus about an opportunity for people just like you! Couples Connect is designed to provide a forum where young families (20s & 30s is the guideline, not the rule) can connect and grow together in Christ. We meet together once a month on Sunday mornings from 9-10am in the Varsity Café.

We hope you’ll join us and see how God will inspire, encourage and strengthen us all as we gather on these Sundays. There is much in store for you … including a great light breakfast. Couples with children, you can drop them off at the nursery or their respective CSM venues before coming to Couples Connect. CSM workers will be aware that children may be dropped off after the typical 8:30 starting time.

Oh, one more thing: Go ahead and invite other young couples you may know to join us! We can’t wait to see you there!
